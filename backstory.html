This is where your detective story starts.

In a near-future world where tech giants like Nexora Systems shape thought through AI and behavioral algorithms, one engineer—Elias Thorn—uncovers a secret protocol capable of rewriting ethical boundaries and even human memory. When he mysteriously dies, Detective Rhea Calder is drawn into a buried conspiracy involving erased logs, ghost code, and hidden messages left behind. As she unravels the truth, it becomes clear that free will itself may be programmable—and Elias might not be as gone as he seems.
